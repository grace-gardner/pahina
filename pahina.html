  • Why Why

Designing your web pages can be costly when hiring a web developer/designer. Apart from paying for the work fee, there also maintenance issues and/or site development costs that you might require on a regularly basis which you will also need to consider in your budget. Start up companies and small businesses especially with low budget normally require just a basic website at first. As such, paying someone a hefty fee to create and design your website is not very incentivising. Sure, there is the option of using website builders but there are also some disadvantages that come along with it. The biggest concern of it all is that your source codes will mainly be stored on their servers and should their servers go down, your website will be affected. You can read more about it on webdesignerdepot.com
But what if you can do it yourself without any third parties involved?

  • Inspire Me Inspire Me

All you need is some basic understanding of HTML and CSS. There are hundreds of video tutorials, blogs and even free online courses that you can utilise to learn HTML and CSS. Once you understand how a web page is structured by HTML and styled by CSS, the rest is up to your imagination. Hence, the creation of this site. I wanted to create an open-source website for anyone who simply want a basic web page for their small/startup business but can't afford to pay web designers or website builders. Here you'll be able to do it yourself for free. I have created a variety of different web designs that are copyright-free so you can copy and recreate them for your own website.
Simply click on the image to go to the sample website and if you like the design and want to use it, come back to this page and watch the tutorial videos of how you can copy and recreate the design.
