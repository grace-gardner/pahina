Create & Design
your own Website

Designing your web pages can be costly when hiring a web developer/designer. Apart from paying for the fee, there are also site maintenance/development that you might require on a regular basis. You will need to factor in these extra costs in your budget. Start up companies and small businesses especially with low budget normally require just a basic website at first. As such, paying someone a hefty fee to create and design your website is not very budget-friendly. Sure, there is the option of using website builders but there are also some disadvantages that come along with it. The biggest concern of it all is that your source codes will mainly be stored on their servers and should their servers go down, your website will be affected. You can read more about it on webdesignerdepot.com where they breakdown in much details atleast 9 reasons why hand-coding your own website is ideal.
But how can you DIY your own website without any third parties involved?

All you need is some basic understanding of HTML and CSS. There are hundreds of video tutorials, blogs and even free online courses that you can utilise to learn HTML and CSS. Once you understand how a web page is structured by HTML and styled by CSS, the rest is up to your imagination. Hence, the creation of this site. I wanted to create an open-source website for anyone who simply want a basic web page for their small/startup business but can't afford to pay web designers or website builders. Here you'll be able to do it yourself for free. I have created a variety of different web designs that are copyright-free so you can copy and recreate them for your own website.
